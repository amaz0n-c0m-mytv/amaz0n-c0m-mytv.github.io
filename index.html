My Tv Enter Code

Amazon My TV Code is a unique 5-6 character code displayed on your TV screen when you attempt to access Amazon. This code is used to link your TV to your Amazon account and activate Amazon Prime Video on your device.

Here's how it works:

  • Open the Amazon Prime Video app on your device (e.g., Smart TV, streaming device, or gaming console).
  • Sign in with your Amazon account credentials (email and password).
  • If prompted, enter the activation code displayed on your TV or device.
  • Go to Amazon.com/mytv on your computer or mobile device.
  • Sign in with the same Amazon account credentials.
  • Enter the activation code and click "Continue".
  • Follow the prompts to complete the activation process.

Steps to create your Amazon My Tv Account

  • Go to Amazon.com/mytv on your computer or mobile device.
  • Click "Create an account" and enter your email address and password.
  • Fill out the registration form with your name, birthdate, and other details.
  • Verify your email address by clicking on the confirmation link sent by Amazon.
  • Sign in to your Amazon account on your TV using the Amazon Prime Video app.
  • Enter the Amazon My TV code displayed on your TV screen.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the activation process.

How to setup Amazon Prime Video on your TV?

  • Turn on Your TV and Connect to the Internet: Ensure your smart TV or streaming device is connected to the internet.
  • Open the Amazon Prime Video App: Locate and open the Amazon Prime Video app on your TV or streaming device.
  • Sign In: You will be prompted to sign in to your Amazon account. If you don’t have the app installed, download it from your device's app store first.
  • Visit Amazon.com/mytv: On your computer or mobile device, open a web browser and go to www.mazon.com/mytv.
  • Enter the Code: Your TV screen will display a unique activation code. Enter this code on the Amazon.com webpage.
  • Complete Activation: Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the activation. Once done, your TV will be linked to your Amazon account.
  • Start Streaming: You can now start streaming movies and TV shows on Amazon Prime Video.

Activate Amazon Prime Video on your TV

  • Open Amazon Prime Video App: Find and open the Amazon Prime Video app on your TV. Download it from the app store if needed.
